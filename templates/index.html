5 Key Innovations

1

Objective AU Detection

Replace subjective CNN emotion classifiers with biomechanical Facial Action Unit analysis via OpenFace 3.0 — culture-independent, scientifically validated.

2

Full Speech Pipeline

Complete parallel audio modality: Pitch, Jitter, Shimmer, MFCCs, and temporal dynamics through 1D-CNN + LSTM architecture.

3

Continuous V-A Regression

No discrete emotion classes. Continuous 2D Valence-Arousal output — the clinical gold standard for affective computing.

4

Cross-Modal Attention

Multimodal Transformer fusion: face features attend to speech features and vice versa — learns emergent cross-modal stress patterns.

5

Personalized Calibration + Federated Learning

90-second neutral baseline calibration per user. Stress detected as deviation from YOUR personal neutral state. All biometric data processed locally via Federated Learning — privacy guaranteed.
